Do’s
•	✔️ Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate 
•	✔️ Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information 
•	✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation 
•	✔️ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication 
•	✔️ Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and values 

Don’ts
•	❌ Do not share sensitive personal details like address, phone number, or passwords 
•	❌ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
•	❌ Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments 
•	❌ Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments 
•	❌ Do not overshare your location or daily routine publicly
